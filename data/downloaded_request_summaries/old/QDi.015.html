Reason for listing:

Mahfouz Ould al-Walid was listed on 6 October 2001 , pursuant to paragraph 8 (c) of resolution 1333 (2000) as being associated with Al-Qaida, Usama bin Laden or the Taliban for “participating in the financing, planning, facilitating, preparing, or perpetrating of acts or activities by, in conjunction with, under the name of, on behalf of, or in support of” Al-Qaida (QDe.004).

Additional information:

Mahfouz Ould al-Walid was a senior Al-Qaida (QDe.004) leader who followed Usama bin Laden (deceased) from Sudan to Afghanistan in the late 1990s. He has performed several roles within Al-Qaida: spiritual leader, legal scholar and operational planner. In 2001, Mahfouz Ould al-Walid was a close associate and strong supporter of the Taliban.
